About Us

Our Story

As a little girl I used to repaint my room countless times, and frequently moved furniture around to get that new look. I loved sewing cushions covers, and every Saturday my parents and I were always going off to the hustle and bustle of local auctions.

My entrepreneurial skills, passion, keen eye for detail and spirit, come my parents, who were also both entrepreneurs and worked extremely hard for everything they achieved. When I became a mum I began designing baby clothing and started my first company LUNDMYR of Sweden. My love for Spain brought me to Marbella in 2012, where I purchased my first holiday apartment. However, I soon discovered that it was difficult to find good, affordable quality furniture, as the selection on the Costa del Sol is either extremely expensive, or budget like Ikea, and not at all as like the furniture we are are used to in Sweden.
